## AWS Pricing Model

### Three fundamental drivers of cost with AWS
1. Compute
- Calculated by the hour or second.
- Varies by instance type.

2. Storage
- Charged typically per GB.

3. Data Transfer
- Outbound is aggregated and charged.
- Inbound has no charge.
- Charged per GB.
